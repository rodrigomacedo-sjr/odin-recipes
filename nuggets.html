Homemade Nuggets

About the recipe

These are a healthier version of a classic dish. The texture may be very unique and not for the faint of hearth.

The first time that I myself made this recipe it almost ended my relationship. I can't say it gets easier, but it sure is rewarding when those actually come out right.

It is an experience that every person should strive to. Once you make and try these, I guarantee your life will never be the same.

Ingredients

  • A whole chicken (yes)
  • An onion that reminds you of an ogre
  • Crumbs of a once loved bread
  • Your favorite cheese
  • Seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven at hellish temperatures
  2. Pray and sacrifice the chicken in the name of your favorite entity
  3. Rip your chicken to pieces with your bare hands. Finish with a properly consecrate knife
  4. Alchemy your way into fusing the destroyed chicken with the onion and the bread crumbs
  5. Manipulate the hauting meat-ish mass into byte size nuggies
  6. Burn it until a miracle happen
  7. Your recipe is ready!

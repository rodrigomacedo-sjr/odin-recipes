Secret Omelet

About the recipe

A good egg is halfway the path of a life changing omelet. The other half if definetly love (and the other secret ingredient)

Legends say a good omelet can save a life. I witnessed this with my own eyes in one of the coldest days in the history of Alaska.

The best tip I can give on succeding on this recipe is believing in yourself. It also really helps to be a good chef. A really good one I mean, michelin stars and shit.

Ingredients

  • A golden eggs chicken alive
  • Milk from a cow that has never seen pain
  • Salt from the Mariana Trench bottom
  • Pepper grown in the inside of an active volcano
  • Tomatoes watered only with tear of joy

Directions

  1. Gently ask the chicken for a couple of its eggs
  2. Peel off the edible gold (it will be use in the finalization)
  3. Emulsify the eggs perfectly with the milk using a hearth shaped fork
  4. Gently add the super salt and super peper
  5. Let it rest for a year (yes)
  6. Decorate it with the whole tomatoes and sprinkle some edible gold on top
  7. Your recipe is ready!

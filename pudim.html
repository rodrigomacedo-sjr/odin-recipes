Mug Pudim

About the recipe

Really simple brazillian recipe, gotta love those. An all time classic I remember making myself as a kid.

It is important to be very careful in the process but enjoy yourself at the same time.

If you are looking to win a cooking championship this must be your go to in the sweet meta. You will be like Faker but in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Condensed milk
  • Whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Water

Directions

  1. Put the sugar and the water on a mug
  2. Microwave it for 30 seconds
  3. In another recipient, toss the eggs and mix them real good
  4. Add the milk and the condensed milk
  5. Mix everything even more
  6. Toss it all on the microwave again
  7. Wait until it's cold (tip: use a refrigetor)
  8. Your recipe is ready!
